Defending our Nation as a Civilian

By Ethan Biggs (Not affiliated with the DoD or official gvmt agency)

Military members are seen as the defenders of our country. In the Airmen's Creed of the Air Force, Airmen proclaim to be "their nation's sword and shield, it's sentry and avenger." For those of us who haven't taken on ourselves such a commitment, then there are still ways to serve out country that don't recquire military service. Getting informed, voting, and working in your community help build up the basic units of society. Also, being informed about what our military does is helpful in being educated and supportive of our nation's warfighters.
